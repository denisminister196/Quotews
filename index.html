These rare moments offer the opportunity to manifest meaningful changes. Yes, the new year is an imagined space in time, but the psychological aspect of it allows us to visualise ourselves according to our ideals, and to project that image into the future. Such visualisations are a powerful instrument for creating real changes, motivating us to step into our better selves. It’s a golden opportunity for self-development – but how many of us actually take full advantage of this potential? This year, let’s stop saying ‘not yet’ to the things we really want to do. Harness the psychological power of the new year to fuel your self-development, and embrace the goals that drive you forwards – no matter how big or small.
